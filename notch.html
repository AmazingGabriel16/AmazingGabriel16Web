I had developed this splashpack as a way of showing thank you to Notch for all those great childhood memories everyone made on Minecraft.

Especially now that Microsoft had removed your name from the original splashscreen, this is the least that the Minecaft community could do for to remember you.

So thank you Notch!
Gab - Minecaft player since 1.2.5

Update 1.14
-  Added Notch.

Recently Microsoft had deleted any mentions of our beloved childhood hero 'Notch' from the splash screens.Because of that I had came up with a plan, to create a texture pack that would include him once again.It only took 3-4 hours but Notch never dies! The texture pack is just the default 1.14 texture pack with added splash texts.

The added splash texts include the following:

- Made by Notch!

- Notch <3 ez!

- The Work Of Notch!

^The above are taken from the .jar file of Minecraft 1.7.2's splash.txt file.
