Possible soultions
Possible solution
1. Use strong but easy to remember password
2. Use two factor authentications
3. Install anti-virus
4. Update or change your password often

Advantages and disadvantages of each
Use strong but easy to remember password

Advantage:
It’s harder to guess

Disadvantage:
You might forget what your password is

Use two factor authentications

Advantage:
If they try to change your password you get a notification

Disadvantage:
It can be turned against to the users
Install anti-virus

Advantage:
 You get protection against the viruses

Disadvantage:
It can slow down your computer or internet
Update or change your password often

Advantage:
People who may have your old password will not be able to get in easily as the passwords would be changed every time.

Disadvantage:
It will become hard to remember your passwords as it is always getting changed.
