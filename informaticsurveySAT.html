Informatics Survey Findings

In the time of June, a survey had been conducted with 27 participants asked questions regarding TV and online streaming services to answer the question if 'Adolescents no longer watch commercial television due to the increasing options of accessibility of online streaming services'.

We had then asked them questions such as what service they use, when they had first used that service, how long they spend on average in one session with that service and lastly what service or channel they used to watch.

Most used type of service
According to the survey, only 2 out of 27 people use TV as their main source of receiving media content whilst 25 out of 27 people use online media streaming services as their main source of receiving media content.

Usage trend
According to the survey, the use of people using online streaming services for the first time has increased largely after it's wide availability between 2012 and 2016. With a decline in the use of people's first time using a TV.

Most used online streaming service
According to the survey, it is found that YouTube is the most used online streaming, probably because it is a free service and Netflix coming into second probably because it is a well known service that provides both original content and a wide range of movies.

Online Average Hrs/Per Session
According to the survey, the average hours people use online services in each session is 1-2 hours.

Most watched TV channel
According to the survey, it was found that Seven was the most popular channel to watch with other child channels of Seven, probably due to it being a channel that provides a large array of content from news to movies to tv shows like The Simpsons, Futurama, movies and original content.

TV Average Hrs/Per Session
According to the survey, the average hours people use online services in each session is 0-1 hours.
