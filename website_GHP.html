Website
Solution suitable for a mobile device.

A solution to get a website mobile friendly is to implement some code that will change the size of the screen displayed depending on the size of the mobile device screen. Another method is to also just use website builders like Wix, Google Sites, Weebly, Wordpress and Mobirise as they automatically implement the code and is instantly ready to be viewed on mobile with most of them having a website preview option.

Discussion of the constraints encountered for a mobile device solution.

Constraints:

- If manually coding the website, it will take long periods of time as each part of the website will need to be tested.

- Prebuilt website builders have only a limited selection of themes and other content that can be implemented. The content may not be one that the user desires.

- Some functions may not be available on mobile and therefore the solution may hide information to users.

- There is no hover state for users as there is commonly no mouse involved and everything is one-clicked.

- There may be slow typing on websites when entering text and other things due to the screen size.

- The website would have less context as it is harder to read on the mobile.

- There may be some inaccurate clicks on the website with a mobile device solution, though this can be fixed easily with a more mobile friendlier website.

- The website may not load properly due to it having different size ratios and so on that displace the elements.

Description of the information system and the issue researched.

- IS= Social Media

- Issue = Security

Social Media:
Social media is a series of websites and applications that enable users to create and share content or to participate in social networking, commonly communication is within friends or around the globe at an international level. Social media services include Facebook, Instagram, Linked In, Snapchat and so much more.

Security:

With the growing use of social media all of the world, there have been many attempts to hack into the systems and compromise people’s confidential information despite the level of security applied to keep our data safe. People have used hacking social media profiles to gain personal information such as birthdays, addresses and other information such as their credit card that is commonly complete to promote crime by impersonating another person. They have used techniques also such as social engineering to gain the better of people mostly young and old who have not lived in the social media era long enough to understand the risks.

Ending:

With social media spreading like wild fire and security weakening as people find new methods, we’ve come up with a well known problem called that can be called and is known as social media hacking. A hack that involves the infiltration of the social media network using new methods, one of them being social engineering and casual hacking.
